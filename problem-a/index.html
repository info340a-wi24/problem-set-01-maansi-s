Rapunzel

From the movie "Tangled", Rapunzel is a Disney princess whose story is about her being kidnapped from her family of royalty by the greedy Mother Gothel. With her magical, long hair that has healing powers, Mother Gothel uses Rapunzel to maintain her youth and "beauty." Since Rapunzel was kidnapped as an infant, she lives her life in naivety and control, but grows up to find the love of her life, reconnect with her family, and leave the curse of Mother Gothel.

This information is based on the Disney movie Tangled, directed by Nathan Greno and Byron Howard (2010).
